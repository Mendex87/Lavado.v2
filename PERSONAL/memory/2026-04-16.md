## Operational design decisions (late April 16)
- Operator must close a process and open a new one if anything changes (line, cantera, mode). No mid-process mutation.
- Two user roles defined: Operator (operates) and Supervisor (administers and corrects).
- Stock is tracked per cantera in tons. Consumed by line entry scales (not output), because water is added during washing so output weight ≠ raw material consumed.
- Product output always has a humidity percentage, used to calculate real cantera yield.
- Future: humidity sensors per product for automatic water correction in yield calculation.
- Future: stock can come from PLC (truck entry scale) instead of manual entry.
- Key KPI: kg of useful product per kg of raw sand from each cantera (cantera yield/rendimiento).
- Stock for now is manual (supervisor loads it); later may be automated from PLC truck scale.
- Codex errors seen in session (model not supported) — unrelated noise, ignored.
- Whisper model "tiny" working for audio transcription but with some word errors; plan to improve model size later.
- Next pending step: PLC variable map — what the app receives from PLC and what it sends to PLC.
